galaxy Voice Assistant - Commands

How to Speak Commands

Start your command with "galaxy", then say what you want it to do. Examples:

  • galaxy, what is time(it gives you exact time)
  • galaxy, what is 50 plus 20 (you can ask any number in +,-,*,/)
  • galaxy, what is table of 2. (you can ask any table under 2 to 20)
  • galaxy, what is date (it gives you exact date)
  • galaxy, open YouTube

Supported Browsers

  • Google Chrome (Recommended)
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (iPhone / iPad)
  • Other Chromium-based browsers
Note: Make sure your microphone is enabled and the browser has permission for voice input. Commands may not work on older browsers.

Tips for Better Recognition

  • Speak clearly and slowly.
  • Always start commands with "galaxy".
  • Minimize background noise.
  • Use simple sentences for math or spellings.
