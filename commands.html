Lucifer Voice Assistant - Commands

How to Speak Commands

Start your command with "Lucifer", then say what you want it to do. Examples:

  • Lucifer, tell me the table of 2
  • Lucifer, what is 50 plus 20
  • Lucifer, tell me four-legged animals' names
  • Lucifer, what day is today
  • Lucifer, open YouTube

Supported Browsers

  • Google Chrome (Recommended)
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (iPhone / iPad)
  • Other Chromium-based browsers
Note: Make sure your microphone is enabled and the browser has permission for voice input. Commands may not work on older browsers.

Tips for Better Recognition

  • Speak clearly and slowly.
  • Always start commands with "Lucifer".
  • Minimize background noise.
  • Use simple sentences for math or spellings.
